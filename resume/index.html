Biography

Xinjun Cai is a Ph.D candidate at the School of Software, Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Prof. Yunhao Liu and Prof. Zheng Yang. He is anticipated to complete his doctoral studies at Tsinghua University and obtain a Ph.D. degree in July 2024.Prior to that, he received his B.E. degree in 2018 from from the School of Computer Science at Beijing Institute of Technology.
His research interests include mobile edge computing, visual SLAM and AR/VR.
